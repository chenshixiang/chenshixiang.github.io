Research

My research interests center on Riemannian optimization, decentralized optimization, and machine learning.

01

Riemannian Optimization

Optimization over Stiefel and related matrix manifolds.

02

Decentralized Optimization

Distributed and consensus-based methods for networked optimization problems.

03

Machine Learning

Optimization methods and learning-based frameworks for machine learning models.
