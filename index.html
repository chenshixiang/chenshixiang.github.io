A brief cv

About me

I am an Assistant Professor(特任研究员) at School of Mathematical Sciences, University of Science and Technology(USTC). My research interests are optimization and machine learning. My recent research focuses on decentralized optimization and Riemannian optimization. I received the 2024 INFORMS Computing Society Prize and the 2024 SIAM Review SIGEST Award.

Research

My research interests include:
