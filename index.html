A brief cv

About me

I am an assisstant professor（(特任研究员） at School of Mathematical Sciences， University of Science and Technology(USTC).

I was a Postdoctoral Associate at Texas A&M University from Sept. 2019 to Aug. 2021, with Prof. Alfredo Garcia and Prof. Shahin Shahrampour.

I completed my PhD at the Chinese University of Hong Kong in 2019, under the supervision of Prof. Shiqian Ma and Prof. Anthony Man-Cho So. I earned Bachelor’s degree in Mathematics at Nanjing University in 2015. My research interests are optimization and machine learning. My recent research focuses on decentralized optimization and Riemannian optimization.
